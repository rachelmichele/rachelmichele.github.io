Tree Trail Boards

Tree Trail Boards is a board design company whose purpose is encourage skateboarders and long boarders to explore the more natural areas of Newark. The board features trails that go into White Clay Creek, a park in Newark, Delaware.

This was a conceptual design.

Project Brief

Switch Skate Boarding's design competition challenged participants to create a skateboard design with its own social media marketing campaign.

Solution

Tree Trail Boards' deck design is a map of popular longboarding routes in Newark, Delaware. There are spots to write in best time and top speeds integrated into the design. Those numbers are the most important metrics in longboard racing. The social media campaign was based around being able to use Snapchat & Instagram to photograph and edit image to show which route you took and type up your speeds and times to share with your friends.
